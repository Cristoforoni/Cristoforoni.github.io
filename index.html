About me

My research focuses on the Analysis of PDEs and Spectral Inequalities, with particular attention to the behaviour of solutions to elliptic problems with thin layers, Shape Optimization, and Free discontinuity problems.

I completed my PhD, in Mathematical and physical sciences for advanced materials and technologies, at the Scuola Superiore Meridionale, Napoli (Italy), in January 2026 under the supervision of Prof. Carlo Nitsch, with a thesis titled "The Robin Boundary Conditions in Variational Problems and Thermal Insulation".
